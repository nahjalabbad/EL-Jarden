Bree / BLACK BAMBOO

Feeding

Keep her growing healthily by giving her a liquid feed in spring and summer. A couple of times a month is enough.

Moist soil

She likes quite moist soil, so water her regularly. Make sure excess water can drain from the bottom of her pot.

Most light conditions

She’s very easygoing and will manage in sun or shade. She’ll grow much faster in a sunny spot.
